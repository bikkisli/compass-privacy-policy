Compass Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 9, 2026

Compass is an internal testing application used by Suntra MedTech to support grounded Jira and Confluence workflows. This Privacy Policy explains what information Compass collects during testing, how that information is used, and how it is protected.

Who is operating Compass

Compass is operated for internal evaluation and testing by Suntra MedTech.

What information Compass collects

  • Your company email address and account identity.
  • Authentication and integration metadata needed to sign you in and connect third-party services.
  • Project context and content you choose to load from Confluence and, if enabled, Google Drive.
  • Generated story drafts, prompts, transcripts, approvals, and workflow history created while using the app.
  • Jira and Confluence actions you explicitly trigger through the application.
  • Usage logs, diagnostic events, and in-app feedback submissions, including optional screenshots.

How Compass uses this information

  • To authenticate users and restrict access to approved internal testers.
  • To connect to Atlassian and optional Google Drive services that you authorize.
  • To generate, review, and refine product requirements, Jira stories, and related workflow outputs.
  • To troubleshoot defects, improve reliability, and evaluate the product during internal testing.
  • To maintain auditability for actions such as approvals, issue creation, and feedback submissions.

Atlassian and Google data

If you connect Atlassian or Google Drive, Compass stores the minimum tokens and related metadata required to keep that connection working. Those credentials are used only to perform the actions you request inside Compass.

Who can access the data

Access is limited to authorized Suntra MedTech personnel and the infrastructure/services used to operate Compass, including Supabase, Render, Atlassian, Google, and Anthropic, as applicable to the workflow you use.

How long data is kept

Testing data may be retained for product evaluation, debugging, auditability, and internal operational needs. Data may be deleted or reset during development and testing cycles.

Your choices

  • You may choose whether to connect optional integrations such as Google Drive.
  • You may choose whether to submit in-app feedback and screenshots.
  • You should avoid entering sensitive information that is not necessary for testing Compass.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or requests related to test data, contact: bsha@suntramedtech.com

This page is provided solely to support internal testing and sharing requirements for the Compass application.
